Frequently Asked Questions
What is Remote Monitoring and how does it work?
We track your vital signs remotely and provide personalized updates, so you stay connected to your health without clinic visits.
How do I subscribe to the Remote Monitoring plan?
Sign up through our website or WhatsApp. The plan is ₦1,000 per month.
What health data will be tracked through Remote Monitoring?
We monitor blood pressure, blood sugar, heart rate, sleep patterns, and more, based on your needs.
Can I book a virtual consultation along with Remote Monitoring?
Yes, you can! Our team is available for virtual consultations. If you need to speak with a doctor or specialist, just send us a request through WhatsApp.
How do I request lab tests or prescriptions?
You can easily request lab tests or prescriptions by contacting us through WhatsApp. Our team will guide you through the next steps and arrange the service for you.
How quickly will I get a WhatsApp response?
We respond immediately during business hours, and as soon as possible after hours.
