Yes. We use end-to-end encryption and follow global best practices to keep your data secure and confidential.

Only you and the health professional you choose to share it with. We never sell or share your data with third parties
How do I sign up?
Download the app from the Play Store or App Store, create an account, and enter your referral code (if you have one).
What can I do with this app?
Yes, you can! You can log blood pressure and blood sugar readings, get insights and reminders, and share progress with your doctor.
How often should I log my readings?
Twice daily logging is recommended for consistent monitoring and better insights, but you can log as often as possible.
